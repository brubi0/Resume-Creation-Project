## Skills Categories

### Core NetSuite Modules

**Record to Report (R2R)**
GL management, journal entries, period close, financial statements (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow), account reconciliations, consolidations (multi-subsidiary/OneWorld), audit trail and compliance.

**Design to Build**
BOMs, work orders, assembly builds, routing and work centers, production planning, manufacturing cost tracking, engineering change management.

**Order to Cash (O2C)**
Sales orders, fulfillment/shipping, invoicing, AR, payment processing and application, RMAs and credits, revenue recognition basics.

**Procure to Pay (P2P)**
Purchase orders, vendor management, receiving/item receipts, vendor bills/AP processing, bill payments, 3-way matching (PO, Receipt, Bill), vendor credits and returns.

**Reporting / Analytics**
Saved searches (building, formulas, summary types), SuiteAnalytics Workbook, financial reports customization, dashboards and KPIs, report builder, data exports and scheduling, cross-subsidiary reporting.

### Add-On Modules

**Advanced Inventory / Manufacturing**
Multiple inventory locations, bin management, demand planning, multi-location transfers, landed cost tracking, advanced lot/serial tracking, manufacturing routing and scheduling.

**Advanced Revenue Management (ARM)**
ASC 606 / IFRS 15 compliance, revenue arrangements and elements, performance obligations, fair value allocation, deferred revenue scheduling, multi-element arrangements.

**E-Commerce (SuiteCommerce / SCA)**
SuiteCommerce Advanced/Standard, web store configuration, product catalog management, shopping cart and checkout, customer-facing portals, themes and customization.

**Fixed Asset Management (FAM)**
Asset records and acquisition, depreciation schedules and methods, asset disposal and write-offs, depreciation posting to GL, asset reports, tax vs. book depreciation, asset transfers.

**Lead to Quote (CRM)**
Lead records and qualification, opportunity management, sales pipeline and forecasting, quote/estimate creation, sales rep assignment, win/loss tracking, CRM dashboards.

**NSPB (Planning and Budgeting)**
Annual budget creation, forecasting and projections, scenario modeling, budget vs. actual reporting, rolling forecasts, departmental budget workflows, driver-based planning.

### Other Skills

**SuiteBuilder**
Custom fields, records, forms, subtabs and sublists, custom lists, roles and permissions configuration.

**SuiteFlow**
Workflow creation and states, transitions and conditions, actions (set field, create record, send email), triggers, approval routing, subflows.

**Advanced PDFs (Freemarker)**
Advanced PDF/HTML templates, Freemarker syntax, invoice/PO/SO templates, conditional logic, looping through line items, BFO rendering.

**SuiteScript (JavaScript)**
SuiteScript 1.0 and 2.x, script types (Client, User Event, Scheduled, Map/Reduce, Suitelet, Restlet), N/ modules, script deployment, debugging.

**Integrations**
Integration platforms (Celigo, Boomi), RESTlet and SOAP web services, token-based auth / OAuth, SuiteTalk, CSV imports, integration monitoring and error handling.

**Training / Enablement**
Training materials and documentation, live training sessions, SOPs, process flowcharts, knowledge base articles, new user onboarding.

**User Adoption**
Change management strategies, adoption metrics, feedback collection, champion/super-user programs, ongoing support, continuous improvement.

**Project Management**
Implementation project planning, timeline and milestone management, stakeholder coordination, resource management, risk mitigation, UAT and go-live planning.

**Data Migration**
Data extraction from legacy systems, data cleaning and validation, field mapping, CSV import templates, import sequencing, historical data decisions, GL balance imports, reconciliation post-migration.
