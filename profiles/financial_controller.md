## Skills Categories

### Core Accounting Functions

**Record to Report (R2R)**
GL management, journal entries, period close, financial statements (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow), account reconciliations, consolidations (multi-entity), audit trail and compliance, intercompany eliminations.

**Order to Cash (O2C)**
Revenue cycle oversight, invoicing, AR management, payment processing and application, credit and collections, revenue recognition, deductions and dispute resolution.

**Procure to Pay (P2P)**
Purchase orders, vendor management, receiving, AP processing, bill payments, 3-way matching (PO, Receipt, Invoice), vendor credits and returns, spend analysis.

**Reporting / Analytics**
Management reporting, financial analysis, dashboard and KPI design, variance analysis, board and investor reporting, ad hoc analysis, data visualization, cross-entity reporting.

### Financial Management

**Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A)**
Budgeting, forecasting, variance analysis, financial modeling, scenario planning, board reporting, long-range planning, capital allocation, profitability analysis, driver-based planning.

**Internal Controls & Compliance**
SOX compliance, internal audit coordination, policy design, risk assessment, segregation of duties, control documentation, regulatory filings, external audit management, GAAP/IFRS compliance.

**Treasury & Cash Management**
Cash flow forecasting, banking relationships, debt management, investment oversight, liquidity planning, working capital optimization, foreign exchange management, covenant compliance.

**Advanced Revenue Management (ARM)**
ASC 606 / IFRS 15 compliance, revenue arrangements and elements, performance obligations, fair value allocation, deferred revenue scheduling, multi-element arrangements.

**Fixed Asset Management (FAM)**
Asset records and acquisition, depreciation schedules and methods, asset disposal and write-offs, depreciation posting to GL, asset reports, tax vs. book depreciation, asset transfers.

**NSPB (Planning and Budgeting)**
Annual budget creation, forecasting and projections, scenario modeling, budget vs. actual reporting, rolling forecasts, departmental budget workflows, driver-based planning.

**Lead to Quote (CRM)**
Lead records and qualification, opportunity management, sales pipeline and forecasting, quote/estimate creation, sales rep assignment, win/loss tracking, CRM dashboards.

### Other Skills

**Team Leadership & Development**
Staff supervision, performance reviews, mentoring, cross-training, department hiring, succession planning, workload management, professional development programs, team building.

**Integrations**
ERP and system integrations, data feeds and APIs, bank integrations, payroll system connections, CSV imports, integration monitoring and error handling.

**Training / Enablement**
Training materials and documentation, live training sessions, SOPs, process flowcharts, knowledge base articles, new hire onboarding for finance teams.

**Project Management**
System implementation planning, timeline and milestone management, stakeholder coordination, resource management, risk mitigation, UAT and go-live planning, process improvement initiatives.

**Data Migration**
Data extraction from legacy systems, data cleaning and validation, field mapping, import templates, import sequencing, historical data decisions, GL balance imports, reconciliation post-migration.
